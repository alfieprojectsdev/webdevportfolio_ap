About Me

I'm a full-stack developer with a background in geodetic data analysis and scientific computing. My transition into web development has been driven by a desire to build practical tools that solve real-world problems for real people.

My approach to development is pragmatic and user-focused. I believe in:

  • Test-driven development - Writing tests first ensures reliability
  • Systematic problem-solving - Breaking complex problems into manageable pieces
  • Clear documentation - Code should be understandable and maintainable
  • Practical solutions - Technology should serve user needs, not the other way around

When I'm not coding, I'm helping my kids with their math homework or exploring ways to make everyday processes more efficient through automation.
